What is Voyage Chartering ?

Voyage Chartering means that a ship owner agrees to carry on board a specific ship a particular cargo for a single voyage from one or more loading ports to one or more discharging ports.

What is Time Chartering ?

Time chartering means that a ship owner provides a designated manned ship to the charterer and the charterer employs the ship for a specific period against the payment of hire instead of certain number of voyages or trips. Time chartering does not include loading/unloading cost in the charter rate.
