Sea Freight

Sea freight is the transportation of goods, consolidated into sea containers via Rotterdam to other seaports or terminals. Onward transportation to an inland terminal may be possible.

Air Freight

Airfreight or Air cargo is the unaccompanied transportation of goods between 2 airports. WBS issues per consignment an Airwaybill with a unique and on the WBS-website traceable number.

Land Freight

Road transport is the international transportation by road, goods are transferred via Distribution-centers and will be delivered at destination without extra local charges.

Voyage Chartering

Voyage Chartering means that a ship owner agrees to carry on board a specific ship a particular cargo for a single voyage from one or more loading ports to one or more discharging ports.

Time Chatering

Time chartering means that a ship owner provides a designated manned ship to the charterer and the charterer employs the ship for a specific period against the payment of hire instead of certain number of voyages or trips. Time chartering does not include loading/unloading cost in the charter rate.
